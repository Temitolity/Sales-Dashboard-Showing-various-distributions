# Sales Dashboard Overview

![Sales Dashboard](./Sales%20Dashboard.PNG)

## Introduction

Welcome to the Sales Dashboard repository! This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales data across multiple dimensions such as country, segment, product, and time. It is designed to help you analyze sales performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

## Dashboard Breakdown

### 1. **Total Revenue and Profit**
   - **Total Revenue**: $118,726,350.30
   - **Total Profit**: $16,893,702.26
   - **Total Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)**: $101,832,648.00
   - **Total Units Sold**: 112,5806

### 2. **Sales by Segment**
   - The sales are categorized into five segments: Channel Partners, Enterprise, Government, Midmarket, and Small Business.
   - The **Midmarket** segment holds the largest share of unit sales at 42%, while **Channel Partners** and **Enterprise** follow with 16% and 15% respectively.

### 3. **Trend of Sales**
   - The trend line shows sales variations over time, with noticeable peaks in the **Government** and **Small Business** segments. These insights can be useful for identifying high-performing sectors.

### 4. **Unit Sold Per Product**
   - **Paseo** is the top-selling product with over 300,000 units sold.
   - **VTT** follows closely behind, while **Amarilla** is the least sold product.

### 5. **Profit Per Product**
   - **Paseo** leads in profitability with a total profit of $13,881,773.47, followed by **VTT**.
